Zero Operators Round 3 · Dual-font pairings · Geist + italic serif

Geist does the headlines.
A serif does the italic.

Nine pairings below — each one is Geist for display + body, with a different serif doing the italic-emphasis work only. The italic face handles: emphasis words in headlines, subheads, pull-quotes, playful asides. Everything else is Geist.

01Pairings

Nine combinations. Pick a voice for the italic.

Each card is a mini landing-page moment: headline (Geist), sub-headline with italic emphasis (serif), body (Geist), a pull-quote (serif italic), and a caption. You'll see very quickly which serif voice matches ZO.
